PDF Merger

A free and easy way to combine PDF files into one PDF in just seconds. No matter what operating system or device you're using, as long as you have an Internet connection and are using a device capable of uploading and downloading files, then you can easily combine PDF files.

  • 1
    Select up to 20 PDF files and images from your computer or drag them to the drop area.
  • 2
    Click on and drag an element to a new spot within the list, and the other items will adjust to fit.
  • 3
    When you are ready to proceed, click "Merge & Download" button to download your merged PDFs to your computer.
